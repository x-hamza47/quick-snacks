Recipe: Mango Smoothie

Author: Abdul Moiz Created on: 02/2004 Comments Save
(4.5/5)
Prep Time 2Hrs
Cook Time 1Hr
Serving 1

This Mango Smoothie is a delicious, refreshing treat that’s perfect for any time of the day, especially during the summer months. Packed with natural sweetness from the mango and a creamy texture from the yogurt and milk, it’s a healthy and satisfying option for a quick breakfast, snack, or dessert. You can customize it by adding other fruits like banana or berries, or making it dairy-free by using plant-based milk and yogurt. Enjoy this tropical delight for a burst of flavor and energy!

Ingredients List

Instructions Step by Step

1

Prepare the Mango: Peel and chop the ripe mango into small chunks, discarding the seed.

2

Blend the Ingredients: In a blender, add the chopped mango, yogurt, milk, honey (or sugar), and vanilla extract (if using).

3

Add Ice: Add the ice cubes to the blender to make the smoothie cold and refreshing.

4

Blend Until Smooth: Blend all the ingredients on high speed until smooth and creamy.

5

Taste and Adjust: Taste the smoothie and adjust sweetness by adding more honey or sugar if needed.

6

Serve: Pour the smoothie into a glass and enjoy immediately!

Cooking Note

To get the best flavor and texture, ensure your mango is fully ripe. A ripe mango will have a sweet, fragrant aroma and will yield slightly to gentle pressure when squeezed. If you prefer a thicker smoothie, you can freeze the mango chunks before blending. Additionally, for a richer taste, consider adding a tablespoon of coconut milk or cream. If the smoothie turns out too thick, simply add a bit more milk to reach your desired consistency. Enjoy the freshness of this tropical drink anytime!
